Debate on Reverse Engineering

Carter: "Reverse engineering ancient devices is a fascinating challenge. It's like conversing with a long-gone civilization."

O'Neill: "Ah, ancient devices! You mean like the Stargate? Or are we talking about my 8-track player?"

Carter: "It's not just about understanding the device. It's also about understanding the science and thought process behind it."

O'Neill: "Oh, you mean we could finally figure out how to program a VCR?"

Carter: "Well, that would be a start, but I was thinking more along the lines of technology that could change the world."
