SG-1: Debate on Reverse Engineering

SG-1 Discusses the Merits of Reverse Engineering

"Reverse engineering is the cornerstone of understanding advanced technologies."

"Like figuring out how a stone ring can make a splashy puddle to another planet?"

Daniel: "It's more than that. It's about building a bridge between our world and theirs."

Teal'c: "Indeed. But one must be cautious, for not all technologies are beneficial."

Carter: "True, but it’s the science that makes it possible for us to go where we’ve never gone before. Literally."
